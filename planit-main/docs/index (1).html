What is PlanIt?

PlanIt is a proprietary freeware event management platform. It is free to use and publicly accessible, but it is not open-source software. The source code is published for transparency, not for redistribution or self-hosting.

PlanIt is built for event organisers who need a complete, professional-grade toolset for managing in-person events — from initial invitation through to real-time check-in and post-event analytics.

Guest Invites
Personal QR codes, wallet cards, printable badges, and RSVP tracking.
QR Check-in
Camera scanning, boarding-pass review, anti-fraud protection, and offline mode.
Seating Maps
Visual drag-and-drop floor plans with per-guest table assignment.
Walkie-Talkie
WebRTC push-to-talk voice communication between all logged-in staff.
Anti-Fraud
Trust scores, duplicate detection, flagging, manager override, and audit logs.
Analytics
Real-time attendance stats, check-in progress, and event reporting.

Who is PlanIt for?

PlanIt is designed for event organisers who create and manage events. Organisers have accounts and access the full dashboard. Guests do not need accounts — they receive a unique invite link and QR code.

RoleWhat they doNeeds an account?
OrganiserCreates events, manages guest lists, configures check-in, views analyticsYes
Staff / Check-inScans QR codes, admits guests, uses walkie-talkiePIN-based staff login (no separate account)
GuestViews their invite, presents QR code at entryNo

Licensing & usage

Proprietary Software

PlanIt is proprietary freeware. It is free to use through the official application at planitapp.onrender.com. You may not redistribute, fork, modify, or self-host PlanIt. The public repository is provided for transparency only.

PlanIt is free to use. There are no paid tiers, subscription fees, or per-event charges. It is operated as a hosted service; all events are run on the official PlanIt infrastructure.
